Here's how it works:

  1. Signal your intention to buy / sell DGX
  2. Once a matching buyer / seller has been found, both parties will be notified
  3. Both parties post their ETH address, and I publizise the escrow account address
  4. The seller sends the agreed amount of DGX to the escrow account
  5. The buyer sends the required amount of ETH, to cover the purchase of the DGX
  6. The agent (myself) verifies and approves the transaction
  7. The balance is released to both seller and buyer

(1) Once both parties have been notified, and 24 hours pass without the successful completion of the transaction, the balance will be refunded. (2) The maximum amount per transaction for unknown parties is $500 USD. (3) For repeat-transactions, that means, the same two parties or ETH accounts, the maximum amount per transaction is capped at $2500 USD. (4) I retain a 2% transaction fee, or minimum $5 USD in order to cover my time and ETH network fees. (5) In order to push for transparency, I prefer to keep all transactions public. If you do not want to publish your main ETH account address here, you may create a new, temporary one on MEW.

Post your buy / sell request

To initiate a new request, state the amount you're looking to buy or sell. Example:
Selling 50 DGX at market rate
Selling 100 DGX at 0.12 ETH / DGX
Selling #T0411-01, 5 DGX
Buying 1000 DGX at 0.115 ETH / DGX, private

If you prefer to keep your ETH address private, please mention it in the comment. Private transactions are handled via email, or chat. The transaction fee is 5%.
